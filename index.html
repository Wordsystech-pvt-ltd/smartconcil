Our Story

With 5 Years of Experience

SmartConcil began as a solution to solve the reconciliation problem of one of our clients: the lack of integration and automation in their operations and financial data.
Our Vision
is to be the technological partner that helps companies interconnect with financial platforms to facilitate communication and access to financial services.
Our Mission
is to be the first choice of financial reconciliation software, offering a modular approach that simplifies financial decisions for our clients and eliminates repetitive manual and error-prone processes.
